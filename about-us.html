Sanjay Krishnan

Sanjay Krishnan is an accomplished product and engineering leader based in Los Altos, California, with expertise in scaling innovative technology from launch to market leadership. His professional experience over 25 years includes Enterprise AI Agents, Cybersecurity, AR/VR, Autonomous driving and robotics, Semiconductors. He has been an engineer, product manager, management consultant, board advisor and mentor to early stage founders.

He holds an MBA from UC Berkeley, MS from USC and B.Tech from IIT, Madras. Sanjay is an active voice in the tech community, having authored articles, given talks and teaching a class at Stanford University on AI Agents.

In his spare time, he enjoys playing tennis and watching British murder mysteries on TV.
